Crossing Buntun Bridge

Published on August 29, 2025

Stretching across the mighty Cagayan River, Buntun Bridge is one of the longest bridges in the Philippines. It connects Tuguegarao City with nearby towns and offers sweeping views of the valley.

At sunset, the bridge becomes a perfect viewing spot for photographers and travelers who want to capture the warm glow reflecting on the river.
