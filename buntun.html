Buntun Bridge

Published on August 30, 2025

Buntun Bridge spans the Cagayan River in Tuguegarao City and is one of the longest bridges in the Philippines. It's not only a key transportation link but also offers panoramic views of the river and surrounding landscapes.

Travel Tips

  • Best to visit during sunrise or sunset for amazing views.
  • Wear comfortable shoes if you plan to walk across.
  • Bring a camera for scenic photos.
