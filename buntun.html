Buntun Bridge Adventure

Published on August 30, 2025

One of the longest bridges in the Philippines, Buntun Bridge offers panoramic views of the Cagayan River and connects Tuguegarao to Solana.

Travel Tips

  • Best to visit early morning or sunset for photos.
  • Wear comfortable shoes if walking across.
  • Bring a camera for scenic shots.
