Buntun Bridge

About Buntun Bridge

Buntun Bridge is one of the longest bridges in the Philippines, offering breathtaking views of the Cagayan River and surrounding landscapes. It connects the city of Tuguegarao with surrounding towns and is a landmark for travelers and locals alike.

Travel Tips

  • Visit during sunrise or sunset for the best photos.
  • Bring a camera to capture the scenic river views.
  • Be cautious when walking along pedestrian areas.
