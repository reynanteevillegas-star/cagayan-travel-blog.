Callao Cave Adventure

Published on August 29, 2025

Nestled in PeÃ±ablanca, Cagayan, Callao Cave is one of the most visited natural wonders of the valley. Its seven chambers offer magnificent rock formations and even a chapel where locals hold weddings and masses.

To get there, you can take a tricycle from Tuguegarao City. The best time to visit is early morning when the sun's rays enter through the cave openings, creating a magical natural spotlight inside.

Travel Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes for climbing stairs.
  • Bring a flashlight or headlamp.
  • Respect the sacred chapel area inside.
