Callao Cave Adventure

Published on August 29, 2025

Callao Cave, located in Peñablanca, is one of the most iconic attractions in Cagayan Valley. Famous for its seven chambers, the cave features cathedral-like rock formations and even a natural chapel.

The best time to visit is in the morning when sunlight streams through the cave’s openings, illuminating the limestone walls in a magical glow.

Travel Tips

  • Wear comfy shoes for climbing stairs.
  • Bring a flashlight or headlamp.
  • Respect the chapel inside.
