Callao Cave Adventure

About Callao Cave

Located in Peñablanca, Cagayan, Callao Cave is famous for its seven chambers and the natural cathedral-like rock formations. A chapel inside the cave is a unique attraction where locals hold weddings and masses.

Travel Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes for climbing stairs.
  • Bring a flashlight or headlamp.
  • Respect the sacred chapel area inside.
