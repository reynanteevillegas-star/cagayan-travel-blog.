Palanan, Isabela

Published on August 29, 2025

A coastal gem accessible by plane or boat, Palanan is rich in biodiversity and history. The town offers stunning beaches, pristine rivers, and welcoming locals.

Travel Tips

  • Check the boat schedule in advance.
  • Bring mosquito repellent for forest and river tours.
  • Respect local customs and environment.
