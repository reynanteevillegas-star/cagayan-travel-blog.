Palanan, Isabela

About Palanan

Palanan, located on the northern coast of Isabela, is a remote town known for its rich biodiversity, pristine beaches, and historical significance. Accessible by plane or boat, it is a hidden gem perfect for nature lovers and history enthusiasts.

Travel Tips

  • Plan your visit in advance due to limited transport options.
  • Bring essentials like food and water for remote areas.
  • Respect the environment and local communities.
