Palanan, Isabela

Published on August 30, 2025

Palanan is a coastal town in Isabela, rich in biodiversity and history. It's accessible by plane or boat and offers pristine beaches, waterfalls, and cultural experiences in a serene environment.

Travel Tips

  • Check transportation schedules carefully; access is limited.
  • Bring enough supplies as stores are few.
  • Respect the natural environment and local communities.
