Palanan, Isabela Adventure

Published on August 30, 2025

Palanan is a hidden coastal gem, accessible by plane or boat. Rich in biodiversity, pristine beaches, and history from World War II.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: dry season (November to May).
  • Pack light and bring essentials if traveling by boat.
  • Respect local communities and nature.
